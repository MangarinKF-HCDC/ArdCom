Who is ArdCom?

Founded by Christian Jude T. Elicor, the company was running for 11 years and counting in distributing quality and affordable PC parts and WiFi vending machines here in Mindanao. ArdCom Electronics was founded in 2012.

CORE VALUES

Empowering Progress, Resourcing the Future: Progressive Excellence, Efficiency & Reliability, and Empowerment.

MISSION

  • We provide efficient business opportunities and essential resources to meet the demands of a modern, tech-driven society.
  • As the world evolves, we bridge the gap by delivering the tools and services necessary for constant adaptation.
  • Our company exists to empower the masses through accessible technology and professional growth.

VISION

  • ArdCom Electronics envisions a future where greater opportunities are accessible to everyone.
  • We strive to become a leading industry force by scaling our operations into a global corporation.
  • Through our growth, we aim to create a significant and lasting impact on the world.
